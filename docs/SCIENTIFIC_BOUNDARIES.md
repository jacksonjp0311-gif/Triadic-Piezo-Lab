# Scientific Boundaries

This package is a benchmarked research simulator and reduced-order modeling workbench. It is not experimental validation of a specific manufactured device and does not establish a universal physical law.

Key boundaries:

- The 3D physical manifold has documented high-frequency mesh-convergence limitations.
- The observer-manifold benchmark is calibrated on the included nominal geometry and tested operating envelope.
- The 4D benchmark is dimensionless reduced-order validation, not direct device calibration.
- Visual photon-pair and dynamical-Casimir-inspired effects are qualitative unless explicitly labeled otherwise.
- Archived benchmark results should be reproduced independently before publication-grade claims are made.
